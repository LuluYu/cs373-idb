Cape Town, South Africa

Description

Cape Town is the second-most populous city in South Africa, after Johannesburg, and the provincial capital and primate city of the Western Cape. The City is famous for its harbour, for its natural setting in the Cape floral kingdom, as well as the such well known landmarks as Table Mountain and Cape Point. Cape Town is located on the shore of Table Bay
